How To Book

Apply

Enter your details in the contact form and I will get back to you with my official wedding packages and check availability for your date.

Recieve Invoice

Once we have confirmed your date is available and you have selected which package you would like, you will recieve your official pro-forma invoice.

Pay Deposit

Once you have paid the 50% deposit your wedding date will officially be booked! After that we will arrange a time and date to meet in person and discuss your wedding day.
